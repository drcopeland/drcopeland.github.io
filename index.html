About Me

Hi, I'm Daniel! I love to think about math ∩ computer science ∩ physics.

In my academic career, I conducted research in quantum algebra, a beautiful field that explores and draws connections between representation theory, low-dimensional topology, non-commutative algebra and geometry, and quantum field theory. I'm also keen on learning algorithms (and learning learning algorithms...), both classical and quantum.

Feel free to contact me at daniel.copeland@gmail.com.
